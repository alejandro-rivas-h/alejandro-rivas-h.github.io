My Third CCS Page

Autumn in Sacco is a beautiful time. Our people are reminded that winter is coming, and everyone starts to prepare. Sometimes, it is a challenging season with a lot of cold, but it also comes with beautiful parties and family gatherings.


This beautiful village is nestled within the Cilento and Vallo di Diano National Park in the Province of Salerno, belonging to the Campania region in Italy.
